The House

A table on the road to the caves.

Travelicious sits on Edakkal Caves Road in Ambalavayal, a little under two kilometres from the climb itself — an open, green-walled dining room with garden seating, built for people who arrive dusty from a trek. Continental plates, wood-fired pizza, steaks, sandwiches and proper coffee, alongside Kerala regulars. Pet friendly, and comfortable for a long lunch.
